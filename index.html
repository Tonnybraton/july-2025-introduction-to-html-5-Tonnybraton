Introduction

Modern web design prioritizes both human readability and machine discoverability. Using HTML5 semantic elements ensures that your web pages are accessible to everyone, including users who rely on assistive technologies.

Accessibility

Why Accessibility Matters

Accessibility ensures that all users, including those with disabilities, can navigate and understand your website's content.

Best Practices

  • Use semantic HTML tags (<header>, <main>, <footer>).
  • Provide alt attributes for images.
  • Use ARIA labels when additional context is needed.

SEO Basics

What is SEO?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the practice of improving your website's visibility on search engines like Google and Bing.

Beginner Tips

  1. Use descriptive, keyword-rich titles and headings.
  2. Write meta descriptions that summarize your page content.
  3. Structure content logically with proper heading hierarchy.
